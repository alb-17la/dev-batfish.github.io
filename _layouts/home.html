Batfish

Batfish is an open source network configuration analysis tool that can guarantee the correctness (planned or current) network configurations. It enables network engineers to rapidly and safely evolve the network, without fear of outages or security breaches.

Batfish was originally developed by researchers at Microsoft Research, the University of California, Los Angeles, and the University of Southern California. Many others have since contributed to it. Batfish is currently maintained and offered as a supported service by Intentionet.
